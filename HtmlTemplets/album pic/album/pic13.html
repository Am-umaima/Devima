Switzerland

Tucked away in the Alps, Switzerland is easily one of the most beautiful countries in the world, no matter when you're visiting. Come wintertime though, it becomes a veritable wonderland, with powdery ski slopes and frozen lakes galore. Not a fan of skiing? A summer visit to Switzerland is always a good idea, where Lake Geneva's picturesque banks and Zurich's badi (natural swimming pools along the lakes and rivers) are waiting to host your next family vacation.
